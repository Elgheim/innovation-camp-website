Eunice Aber Betty

Eunice is 15 and in the 8th grade, she wants to become a teacher. It used to take her half an hour to walk to school every morning. The school she went to had a thatched roof that would leak when it rained, and had no toilets. She appreciates her new school with a nice building and clean toilets. Helping her achieve her dream of becoming a teacher.
